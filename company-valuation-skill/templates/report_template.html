4. Methodology

4.1 Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)

Projects future free cash flows over {{ projection_years }} years, discounted at WACC ({{ wacc }}%). Two terminal value methods are computed: Gordon Growth Model (perpetuity growth at {{ terminal_growth }}%) and Exit Multiple ({{ exit_multiple }}x EV/EBITDA). The blended average of both approaches is used as the primary DCF estimate.

4.2 Comparable Company Analysis (CCA)

4.3 Precedent Transactions
