4.1 Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)

Projects future free cash flows over 5 years, discounted at WACC (5.6%). Two terminal value methods are computed: Gordon Growth Model (perpetuity growth at 2.5%) and Exit Multiple (128.1x EV/EBITDA). The blended average of both approaches is used as the primary DCF estimate.

Applies median valuation multiples (EV/EBITDA, EV/Revenue, P/E, P/B) from 4 peer companies to the target's latest financial metrics to derive implied equity value ranges.
