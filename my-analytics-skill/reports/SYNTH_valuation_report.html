4. Methodology

4.1 Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)

Projects future free cash flows over 5 years, discounted at WACC (11.5%). Two terminal value methods are computed: Gordon Growth Model (perpetuity growth at 2.5%) and Exit Multiple (20.8x EV/EBITDA). The blended average of both approaches is used as the primary DCF estimate.

4.2 Comparable Company Analysis (CCA)

Applies median valuation multiples (EV/EBITDA, EV/Revenue, P/E, P/B) from 4 peer companies to the target's latest financial metrics to derive implied equity value ranges.

4.3 Precedent Transactions

Uses multiples from 5 historical M&A transactions in the Technology sector. Transaction multiples inherently include a control premium (avg. 39%), so they typically produce higher valuations than public market comparable analysis.
