4.1 Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)

Projects future free cash flows over 5 years, discounted at WACC (10.4%). Two terminal value methods are computed: Gordon Growth Model (perpetuity growth at 2.5%) and Exit Multiple (30.9x EV/EBITDA). The blended average of both approaches is used as the primary DCF estimate.

4.2 Comparable Company Analysis (CCA)

Applies median valuation multiples (EV/EBITDA, EV/Revenue, P/E, P/B) from 4 peer companies to the target's latest financial metrics to derive implied equity value ranges.

4.3 Precedent Transactions
